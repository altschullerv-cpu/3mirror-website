Layer One

Reverse Generative Calibration

The measurement foundation

A text-to-speech model without emotional instructions cannot diverge from semantics — its output is a pure function of the input text. This makes synthetic speech a natural zero point on the prosodic-semantic divergence scale. RGC constructs a calibration matrix from this zero point: a universal reference that works from the first second of interaction with any new speaker, requiring no prior personal data.

Layer Two

3Mirror

The analysis engine

3Mirror processes semantic and acoustic layers in parallel without fusion. It measures divergence between what is said and how it is said at word-level granularity — with millisecond-precision temporal alignment. The divergence signal is the diagnostic output, not a byproduct.
