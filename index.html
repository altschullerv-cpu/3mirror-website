01 The analysis engine

3Mirror

Word by word. Millisecond by millisecond.

3Mirror listens to two things at once: the words you say, and the way you say them. Most voice AI systems merge these into a single signal and lose the information. 3Mirror keeps them separate — because the gap between meaning and delivery is exactly what carries diagnostic value. 02 The measurement foundation

Reverse Generative Calibration

A universal zero point for prosody.

Before you can measure divergence, you need a zero point. RGC creates that zero point. A text-to-speech model reading words without emotion has no internal state — it cannot diverge from the text. That makes its output a perfect neutral reference. RGC builds a calibration matrix from this reference: a universal baseline that requires no prior recordings of any speaker.

Zero-point reference
Synthetic neutral speech defines the measurement baseline.
Universal
No prior data needed, works from the first second with any speaker.
Reproducible
Same text always yields the same reference point.
Privacy-first
No personal voice data is collected or stored.
03 Where it applies

Applications

Wherever the gap between meaning and delivery carries information — from clinical signal to behavioral integrity to communicative skill.

Deepfake Detection
Synthetic voices are acoustically perfect but behaviorally flat. RGC detects the absence of natural internal-state variation — a signal that survives improvements in generative model quality.
Voice AI
A background layer that tells conversational AI systems what the transcript alone cannot: whether the speaker's tone matches their words.
Clinical Diagnostics
Prosodic patterns change before other symptoms appear. A reproducible measurement scale makes longitudinal tracking possible.
Psychotherapy Support
When a client says "I'm fine" but sounds otherwise, 3Mirror quantifies the divergence. Not a diagnosis — a calibrated signal for the clinician.
HR & Negotiations
High-stakes conversations carry information in how things are said, not just what. Divergence analysis makes that signal measurable.
Communication Training
Using the calibration matrix in reverse — synthesize how a phrase should sound for a specific communicative goal, then train toward it.
